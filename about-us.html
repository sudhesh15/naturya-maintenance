ABOUT NATURYA

NATURYA is a rapidly-growing, ISO 9001:2015 Certified Manufacturer and Wholesale Distributor of Fresh Foods based out of Udupi, Karnataka, India. We cater to both, Retail and Hospitality industry (HoReCa). Naturya is committed to delivering high-quality, fresh and flavourful food products to consumers worldwide. We take pride in offering only 100% natural, preservative-free and additive-free foods. Our product range is wide, varied and ever growing. See photos to know more. Naturya ranks high on product quality, innovation, integrity, customer focus, sustainability and excellence. Call us if you’re looking for a profitable business within or outside India. We are currently offering distributorship for select products.

Our Farmer Partners

The Roots Behind Every Coconut We Process



At Naturya Fresh Foods, we believe the foundation of a great product lies in the integrity of its source. That’s why our journey begins not in a processing unit, but in the lush coconut groves along the fertile coastal belt of Udupi, Karnataka. We take immense pride in sourcing coconuts directly from farmers — not through middlemen or traders, but through long-standing relationships built on trust, fairness, and shared values. Our approach is simple yet powerful: empower the hands that nurture the soil, and the result is a product that’s naturally superior in quality, taste, and purity.


For too long, traditional farmers have been left behind in a supply chain dominated by price squeezes and delayed payments. At Naturya, we are changing that narrative. We work directly with small and mid-sized coconut farmers, ensuring that they receive fair prices for their hard work and timely payments that support their livelihood. By eliminating intermediaries, we give farmers their rightful share and provide them with the confidence to invest in sustainable practices. This model not only benefits farmers but also ensures that our customers receive coconuts that are fresh, mature, and processed within hours of harvesting.


Our farmer network isn’t just a supply chain — it’s a community. We engage with our farmers regularly, visit their farms, understand their seasonal challenges, and provide insights on post-harvest handling to ensure maximum yield and minimum waste. Most of our coconut partners come from coastal and semi-coastal regions known for their nutrient-rich soil and traditional farming methods that rely on nature rather than chemicals. We respect these time-honored practices and never ask our farmers to compromise on natural farming for the sake of yield or speed. In fact, it’s these very practices that make Naturya’s products stand out in purity, flavor, and nutritional value.


One of the key advantages of sourcing directly is traceability. At Naturya, every coconut that enters our facility can be traced back to the farm it came from. This traceability ensures transparency, builds consumer trust, and reinforces accountability at every step. Whether it’s our fresh frozen grated coconut, coconut chunks, pulp, or any of our ready-to-cook products, the raw material is always of the highest quality — thanks to the direct farmer connect that we’ve nurtured over the years. We firmly believe that when you know where your food comes from, you appreciate it more — and so does your body.


Naturya’s partnership with farmers goes beyond buying produce. During times of crop loss, market fluctuations, or natural disruptions, we stand by our farmer partners, often helping them find alternate ways to use their coconuts or offering logistic support to reach our facility. In return, our farmers stay loyal to us, ensuring a consistent supply of mature coconuts even during tight seasons. This mutual commitment is what sets Naturya apart in the coconut industry — a sense of shared responsibility and collective growth. Our success is their success, and vice versa.


If you're a coconut farmer who shares our vision of providing 100% natural and preservative-free food to the world, we’d love to connect. Naturya is always open to expanding its network of trusted farmer partners. Whether you’re a seasoned coconut grower or a next-gen farmer taking over the family grove, reach out to us.
