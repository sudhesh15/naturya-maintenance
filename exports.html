Exports

Bringing the Taste of Coastal Udupi to the World


At Naturya Fresh Foods, we’re proud to extend the rich flavors and exceptional quality of our coconut‑based and South Indian specialty products to markets across the globe. Our Export Services page is designed to give you a clear understanding of how we take every step—from farm to port—to ensure that your overseas customers receive only the freshest, most authentic, and 100% natural foods. Whether you’re an importer, distributor, food‑service operator, or retail chain, our turnkey export solutions are tailored to meet your unique requirements, backed by industry‑leading certifications, end‑to‑end logistics, and unwavering commitment to sustainability.


Comprehensive Product Portfolio
We offer a diverse range of coconut‑derived products and traditional South Indian staples, each processed, frozen, and packaged under strictly controlled conditions:


Fresh Frozen Coconut Varieties

Grated Coconut, Shredded Coconut, Slices, Chunks, Cuts, and Pulp—perfect for confectionery, beverages, cosmetics, and culinary uses.

Ready‑to‑Cook Batters & Chutneys

Authentic Idli & Dosa Batters fermented to perfection, and preservative‑free Coconut Chutney straight from the Udupi coast.

Private‑Label & Bulk Options

Flexible MOQ, customized texture and cut‑size specifications, retail‑ready pouches, bulk cartons, and food‑service drums.

Our packaging formats are engineered for export resilience: multi‑layered polybags, vacuum sealing, and insulated cartons that safeguard product integrity and extend shelf life.


Quality Assurance & Global Compliance
Naturya Fresh Foods adheres to the highest international food‑safety standards. Our facilities are ISO 9001:2015 and HACCP certified, and we comply with FSSAI, EU, FDA, and other regulatory protocols. Every batch undergoes:


Farm‑to‑Factory Traceability

Microbial, moisture, and sensory analyses guarantee that all exports surpass global benchmarks.

In‑House Laboratory Testing

Authentic Idli & Dosa Batters fermented to perfection, and preservative‑free Coconut Chutney straight from the Udupi coast.

Phytosanitary & Customs Documentation

Complete paperwork, origin certificates, and lab reports accompany each shipment, simplifying clearance and reducing delays.

End‑to‑End Logistics & Cold‑Chain Management
Our export prowess is built on seamless logistics and robust cold‑chain systems:


Blast‑Freezing Technology

Rapid freezing locks in freshness so flavors remain as vivid as the moment of harvest.

Temperature‑Controlled Storage & Transport

From −18 °C storage warehouses to reefer containers, we monitor conditions at every link.

Trusted Global Carriers

Partnerships with leading freight forwarders and consolidators ensure reliable, on‑time delivery to major ports in the Americas, Europe, Middle East, Southeast Asia, and beyond.

You’ll receive real‑time shipment tracking and dedicated export coordinators to handle customs queries and scheduling.


Sustainability & Ethical Sourcing
Our export operations mirror the same social responsibility ethos as our domestic business. By sourcing coconuts directly under fair‑trade principles, we empower farming communities, promote agro‑ecological practices, and reduce middlemen. Coconut shells are repurposed for renewable energy, and husk byproducts are offered to partner industries—minimizing waste and strengthening local economies. When you choose Naturya, you align your brand with a genuine, eco‑friendly, and socially conscious supply chain.


Partner with Naturya for Global Success From pilot shipments to full‑scale export programs, we collaborate closely with you at every stage: recipe customization, co‑branding, packaging design, and regulatory alignment. Our flexible minimum‑order quantities and private‑label services make it easy for emerging brands and established players alike to venture into new markets.
