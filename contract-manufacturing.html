Contract Manufacturing Services


At Naturya Fresh Foods, we understand that bringing exceptional coconut and South Indian specialty products to market requires a partner who combines deep-rooted expertise with uncompromising quality. Our contract manufacturing division is built on decades of hands‑on experience in sourcing, processing, and packaging 100% natural, preservative‑free foods. Whether you’re a startup looking to launch your own private‑label brand or a multinational corporation planning large‑scale distribution, our streamlined operations ensure that your products meet the highest standards of purity, taste, and safety. By partnering directly with our facility, you gain access to end‑to‑end manufacturing solutions—from raw material selection in the Udupi coast to final packaging—backed by rigorous quality controls and certifications such as ISO 9001:2015 and HACCP compliance.


Our versatile production lines are designed to accommodate a broad spectrum of coconut‑based and traditional South Indian specialties:

Grated Coconut & Shredded Coconut

Freshly harvested, finely grated or shredded to your specified texture and moisture content.

Coconut Slices, Chunks & Cuts

Uniformly sized pieces ideal for culinary applications, desserts, and ready‑to‑eat snacks.

Coconut Pulp

Nutritious pulp for dairy‑free beverages, cosmetics, and confectionery.

Idli Dosa Batter

Authentic, hand‑crafted batter fermented to perfection, ready for steaming or griddle cooking.

Coconut Chutney

Traditional Udupi‑style chutney, preservative‑free, requiring only thawing and seasoning for instant serving.

Each product line can be tailored in terms of texture, packaging format, and shelf‑life requirements, ensuring a customized solution that aligns with your brand positioning and consumer expectations.


Serving both domestic markets and export destinations worldwide, Naturya’s contract manufacturing services are geared towards versatility and scalability. Our facility boasts state‑of‑the‑art processing equipment, cold‑chain storage, and blast‑freezing tunnels that preserve freshness and extend shelf life. We adhere strictly to FSSAI standards, and our export protocols include compliance with phytosanitary regulations, custom labeling, and multi‑layered packaging to withstand long‑haul shipping. Whether you need bulk shipments of frozen grated coconut for an overseas distributor or retail‑ready pouches of idli dosa batter for domestic supermarkets, our logistics team ensures on‑time delivery through trusted carriers and temperature‑controlled transport.


Collaboration is at the heart of our contract manufacturing philosophy. From initial formulation and pilot trials to full‑scale production runs, our R&D and quality‑assurance teams work closely with you to refine recipes, optimize processing parameters, and conduct in‑house testing for microbial safety, moisture content, and sensory attributes. We offer private‑labeling services, flexible minimum‑order quantities, and bespoke packaging solutions—from retort pouches and stand‑up bags to bulk cartons and food‑service drums. Transparent communication, regular production reports, and batch‑wise traceability empower you to maintain complete oversight of your product journey without having to invest in additional infrastructure.


Choosing Naturya means partnering with a socially responsible manufacturer committed to sustainable sourcing and community upliftment. Our coconuts are procured directly from farmer partners in the Udupi coastal belt, ensuring fair prices, swift payments, and ongoing support for agro‑ecological farming practices. This farm‑to‑factory traceability not only safeguards product authenticity but also resonates with consumers who value ethical supply chains. Through waste‑minimization initiatives—such as utilizing coconut shells for renewable energy and offering coconut husk byproducts to partner industries—we maintain an eco‑friendly footprint across all stages of production.


Whether you’re exploring contract manufacturing for a new coconut‑based line or seeking a reliable partner to expand your current offerings, Naturya Fresh Foods is poised to deliver excellence at every turn. Reach out to our dedicated contract manufacturing team to discuss your project requirements, request a quote, or schedule a facility tour.
