Chunda (Shredded Sweet Mango)

Product Details

  • Product Name: Chunda (Shredded Sweet Mango)
  • Category: Pickle
  • Net Weight: 350g & 650g
  • Product Description:

    Chunda pickle in the Udupi Brahmin style is a delectable condiment that captures the essence of South Indian flavors. Originating from the culinary traditions of the Udupi region, this pickle showcases the perfect balance between sweet, tangy, and spicy notes. Made primarily from grated raw mangoes, jaggery, and an assortment of traditional spices, the Udupi Brahmin chunda pickle is a culinary masterpiece that reflects the careful craftsmanship and attention to detail inherent in this cuisine. The pickle's vibrant yellow hue, derived from the natural blend of turmeric and mango, hints at the harmonious marriage of taste and aesthetics. Served as an accompaniment to a myriad of dishes, the Udupi Brahmin chunda pickle adds a burst of flavor that elevates any meal, embodying the time-honored culinary traditions of the Udupi Brahmin community in every delightful spoonful.
